# Privacy Policy for SunoAuto Ultimate

**Last updated: January 28, 2026**

## Overview

SunoAuto Ultimate is a browser extension designed to automate music creation and downloading on Suno.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Access

| Data Type | Purpose | Stored? |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Song titles | Display in download list | No (temporary) |
| Audio file URLs | Enable MP3/WAV downloads | No (temporary) |
| User preferences | Language, panel position | Yes (locally) |

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Financial or payment information
- Authentication credentials or passwords
- Browsing history outside of Suno.com
- Location data
- Keystrokes or mouse movements

## Data Storage

All user preferences are stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API. No data is ever transmitted to external servers owned or operated by us.

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Transfer your data for purposes unrelated to the extension's functionality
- Use your data for credit determination or lending purposes

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `downloads` | To save MP3 and WAV music files to your device |
| `storage` | To save your preferences (language, panel position) locally |
| `host_permissions` | To access Suno.com and its API servers for downloading music |

## Third-Party Services

This extension interacts only with:
- **Suno.com** - The music generation platform
- **studio-api.prod.suno.com** - Suno's API server
- **cdn1.suno.ai** - Suno's content delivery network

We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
